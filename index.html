Intro

My name is Fotih Turopov, and I'm from Northeastern Ohio. Growing up in the suburbs of Cleveland during the rising digital age of the 2010's, I found myself drawn to the internet due to its expansive possiblities for creative and entertainment outputs. Alongside the heavy effect of videogames like UNDERTALE and Hearts of Iron IV on my life, I knew my path would lie in the digital world.

Right now, I'm an aspiring college student at Case Western Reserve University, majoring in a Computer Science BS with a minor in Business Management. I have a focus on software engineering, artificial intellegence, and machine learning, respectively. For more information on what I get up to, check out my projects.
